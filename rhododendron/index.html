Pacific Rhododendron

Quick Facts

  • Common name: Pacific rhododendron
  • Alternative Names
    • Coast rhododendron
    • California rhododendron
    • California rosebay
    • Informal: rhodie, rhodies
  • Scientific name: Rhododendron macrphyllum
    • Previously: Rhododendron californicum
  • Size range: 5-25 feet (1.524 - 7.62 meters)
  • Geographic range
    • Between Pacific coast and Cascade Mountains
    • British Columbia to Northern California
    • Range from coastal areas up to elevations of 6,000 feet (1.8288 kilometers)
    • In Washington State: Common in Olympic Peninsula and Cascade Mountain range
  • Climate range: Thrives in damp conditions, but tolerates dry. Prefers well-draining, acidic, low-nitrogen soils. Found abundantly in pacific coastal habitats within its range.
