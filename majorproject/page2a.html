The shepherd boy laughed and laughed at the villagers when he saw they fell for his prank. The next day, the shepherd boy grew bored again. He remembered his prank, and how much fun he had. "Wolf! Wolf!" he cried again, and again the villagers ran out to scare the wolf away. But still there was no wolf, and now the villagers were very angry with the boy. Did the shepherd boy care that he had made everyone angry?
