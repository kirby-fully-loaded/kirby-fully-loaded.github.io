The shepherd boy was embarrassed that his music was so bad it could scare away a big, mean wolf. The next day he left his flute at home, and only took his book to the pasture. When he got bored, he took his book out of his pack and soon got too interested in the story to look up. With no scary music and nobody keeping watch, the wolf was able to get three of the shepherd boy's sheep before he noticed the wolf attacking!
