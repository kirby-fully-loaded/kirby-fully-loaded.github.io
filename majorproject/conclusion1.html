The shepherd boy didn't care that he had angered the villagers, and laughed even harder at them because they had fallen for the same trick twice! The villagers stomped back to their homes. On the third day, a wolf really did appear, and began attacking the shepherd boy's sheep! "Wolf! Wolf!" The shepherd boy cried a third time. But the villagers wer still thinking of the past two days. Nobody came to help, and the wolf killed many of the shepherd boy's sheep that day.
