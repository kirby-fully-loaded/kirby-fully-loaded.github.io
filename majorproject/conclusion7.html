The shepherd boy knew his flute playing was bad, but he also knew he had scared the wolf away from his sheep. The next day he took his flute again to keep practicing, keeping careful watch for wolves. Every time he saw a wolf come out of the woods, he'd play a loud, awful note and scare them away, all on his own! His playing improved over the weeks, but he never forgot how to scare the wolves away with just a single note from his flute.
