Simple Pasta

A simple, flexible recipe that tastes great and won't break the bank!

You Will Need

  • Waffle iron (electric)
  • 2 large mixing bowls
  • Electric mixer
  • Measuring spoons (teaspoon, tablespoon)
  • Rubber spatula
  • Optional: small pot

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups whole wheat flour (pastry or regular)
  • 3 eggs, separated
  • one 8-ounce box butter
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 5 tablespoons butter
  • Optional: 1 tablespoon pure maple syrup or honey
  • Optional: choice of fruit

Directions

  1. Prefeat the waffle iron.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. In a separate large bowl, with an electric mixer, beat the egg whites until they form soft peaks. Melt the butter and syrup together (a small pot on the stove works well).
  3. Make a well (hole) in the center of the flour mixture and use a fork to stir in the milk, egg yolks, and melted syrup/butter mixture untill well combined. Lumps in the batter are okay. Carefully fold in the egg whites using a rubber spatula.
  4. Cook in the waffle iron according to the manufacturer's directions. Serve warm wuth warm maple syrup and fresh fruit--blueberries are an excellent choice!
